## ğŸ“ˆ Hashtag Performance Tracking

### **Metrics to Monitor:**
- **Reach**: How many people saw your post
- **Impressions**: How many times your post was seen
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares per hashtag
- **Profile Visits**: From hashtag clicks
- **Follower Growth**: From hashtag discovery

### **Testing Strategy:**

**Week 1-2: Test Phase**
- Try different hashtag combinations
- Use 3 sets of hashtags (A, B, C)
- Track which performs best

**Week 3-4: Optimize Phase**
- Use winning hashtag set 80% of time
- Continue testing with 20% of posts
- Replace underperforming hashtags

**Month 2+: Refine Phase**
- Build your branded hashtag community
- Focus on engagement over reach
- Create hashtag campaigns

### **Instagram:**
- Use all 30 hashtags
- Mix large, medium, small hashtags
- Put hashtags in first comment (optional)
- Update hashtag set monthly

### **TikTok:**
- Use 4-6 hashtags
- Always include 1-2 trending hashtags
- Use niche hashtags for targeted reach
- #FYP and #ForYou are essential

### **Twitter:**
- Use only 2-3 hashtags
- More hashtags = lower engagement
- Make hashtags part of sentence
- Create campaign-specific hashtags

### **YouTube:**
- Use 3-5 hashtags in description
- First 3 appear above video title
- Add 15-20 tags in metadata
- Use exact phrases people search

### **Facebook:**
- Use 1-3 hashtags only
- Focus on content over hashtags
- Use hashtags for campaigns
- Works better in groups

### **LinkedIn:**
- Use 3-5 professional hashtags
- No casual/fun hashtags
- Focus on industry terms
- Use hashtags in articles
