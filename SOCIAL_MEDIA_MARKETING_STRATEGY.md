## ðŸŽ¨ Content Creation Tips

### Visual Branding:
1. **Consistent Colors**: Use your app's color scheme (purple, green, yellow)
2. **Templates**: Create Canva templates for quick posts
3. **Fonts**: Use consistent, readable fonts
4. **Logo**: Always include subtle branding

### Video Content:
1. **Hook in First 3 Seconds**: Start with a question or bold statement
2. **Captions**: Always add captions for accessibility
3. **Quality**: Good lighting, clear audio, steady shots
4. **Length**: 
   - Reels/Shorts: 15-30 seconds
   - TikTok: 15-60 seconds
   - YouTube: 8-15 minutes

### Engagement Tactics:
1. **Call-to-Actions**: 
   - "Download the app link in bio"
   - "Try this quiz and comment your score"
   - "Tag a friend who needs this"
   - "Save this for later"

2. **Interactive Elements**:
   - Polls and quizzes
   - Questions in captions
   - Contests and giveaways
   - User-generated content campaigns
